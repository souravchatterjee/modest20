Essential checklist:

  1. Visa support form:

    If you need a visa to travel to India, please submit the Visa Support Form at your earliest convenience. Please refer to the "Information about Visa" section on the Travel Information page for more visa-related information.

  2. Registration:

    1. Pay the fees. Consult the fee structure to calculate your contribution. Please transfer your payments via NEFT (in case transferring within India) or SWIFT (in case transferring internationally). For international transfers you may also avail other trusted remittance services to Indian accounts if you want. Please consult the account details here. Please make sure that the amount in Indian National Rupee (INR) credited to the conference account is correct.

    2. Once the funds are transferred, please note down the transfer reference number. You will need to mention this in the registration form. Please fill and submit the Registration Form. We will send you a confirmation email about your registration which will include a receipt of all items you have selected.

  3. Abstract submission:

    If you want to submit an abstract, please submit using the Abstract Submission Form. After registration is complete, you will receive a receipt containing your unique registration ID. You will need this ID to complete the abstract submission form.

  4. Hotel reservation:

    We are working with the best hotels near TIFR for special discounted rates for the coference attendees. The list of hotels, their special conference rates, and links to directly book your stay will be available in the "Accommodation" section of the Travel Information page.
