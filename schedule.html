Schedule:

Below is the broad schedule that marks the overall topics and their sessions for the conference. For the detailed list of speakers can be found here. All talks are half an hour in duration, i.e., 20 minutes + 10 minutes for questions. There is also a session where poster presenters can briefly talk about their work. The format should be of an advertisement rather than detailed talks. The duration for these will be 5 minutes, no questions. We have a large session for this. If we finish early, we will use this for discussions. There are several other scheduled times for free-format discussions. Feel free to suggest other times for discussions. The sunset is expected to be around 18:30 hrs and it is usually great to walk out by the sea around that time.
